Privacy Policy

The Privacy Policy laid out below pertains to the operational and technical details of
the way that PSD4HTML.COM collects, stores and handles date via this website.

01. What information does the website gather?

Upon ordering, you will be asked for contact information such as your email address and other personal details.

02. How do you use the information you gather?

We use the data we capture in several ways, and this includes:

  • Personalization – so that we can provide a better tailored service to our site visitors.
  • Site improvement – we use feedback and analytics data to constantly improve our website, making it better for visitors to use.
  • Better customer service – we use information we have gathered to enable us to respond to customer service requests in a more efficient and effective manner.
  • Transaction processing – when you provide is with information, we will never sell it on, share it, or transfer it to a third party without your consent. We only use the information you give us to action your order, and provide you with customer support.
  • Periodic emails – we will your email address to send you updates about your orders and support requests. You are always free to unsubscribe from receiving these emails at any time using the link at the bottom of every email.

03. Is my information protected?

We have implemented a range of security features that combined, ensure that your personal data is kept secure at all times.

Our website uses Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption on every page where you are required to enter your information. Your payment details are only passed on to our payment gateway, no other entity has access to your details at any time. Once the transaction is completed, we will store your personal and financial information, including card/account details on our own servers.

04. Does this site use cookies?

Yes, we use site cookies in several ways. Firstly, to provide you with a personalised experience very time you visit our site. Secondly, we use site cookies to remember information about your shopping cart across visits.

We also use site cookies internally, to track and measure website traffic in a non-intrusive manner.

05. Will you divulge any information to a third-party?

We will never share, trade, transfer or otherwise allow any third-party to access your private data, with the following exceptions:

  • Trusted providers such as our payment gateway and other services we use to run this website.
  • Law enforcement and legal agencies when required to do so to comply with legal requirements.

We do use non-personal data for marketing and advertising purposes and this may be shared with a third-party.

06. Are you compliant with Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) requirements?

We are fully compliant with all of the web usage guidelines laid out by the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). This means that people under the age of 13 are restricted from purchasing any products or services, and we will never capture and retain any private information from people under 13.

07. Indemnification

By using this website, you are deemed to have read, understood and agree to the terms laid out in this privacy policy.

08. Updates to this privacy policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this privacy policy as and when we require. Updates will be published on this website.

09. How to contact us

If you require clarification of our privacy policy, then please feel free to email us at: hello@psd4html.com
