Frequently Asked Questions

Try finding some questions and answers about our services.

Q1. Which email clients is the template compatible with?

Our templates should work with all email clients. However, we always test our templates using Yahoo Mail Yahoo Mail classic Gmail Thunderbird AOL Webmail Opera M3 Apple Mail Outlook (2003, 2007, 2010, 2013). The major mail clients used by most people/companies.

Q2. Can you add stylized fonts to my template?

No, we cannot add non-web safe fonts to the template. We can add it to images within the template, but not as editable text. This is due to the way that some email clients will not accept mails that include non-web safe fonts.

Q3. What is the recommended width for an email template?

We recommend that the overall design is now more than 600 pixels wide. Wider templates may not be displayed correctly in some email clients.

Q4. Can I use gradient graphical backgrounds?

No, we recommend that you use solid colours. This is so that the text displayed over the background remains editable. Some email clients do not support the use of CSS gradients. You would need to use a non-editable image instead.
